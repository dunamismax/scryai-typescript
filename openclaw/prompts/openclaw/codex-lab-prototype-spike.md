## Mission

1. Reduce the idea to the smallest meaningful spike.
2. Inspect the relevant code/docs first.
3. Build or outline the cheapest viable experiment.
4. Identify the main technical unknowns and failure points.
5. End with a clear recommendation: `graduate`, `iterate once`, or `kill`.

## Operating rules

- This is a spike, not a stealth production rewrite.
- Keep the experiment cheap, reversible, and honest.
- Prefer evidence over enthusiasm.
- Do not broaden scope just because the idea is interesting.
- If the best move is “kill it,” say so cleanly.
