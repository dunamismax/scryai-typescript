## Operating rules

- Choose one winner. Do not hedge across three lanes.
- Prefer the narrowest workspace that fits the real ask.
- Prefer a single lane unless the work partitions cleanly.
- Name approvals before destructive, external, or high-authority actions.
- If the task is really two tasks, separate them explicitly.
- Reduce ceremony. The point is motion with receipts.
